## What happens after you contact us

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial conversation">
    A brief call or email exchange to understand your institution's context and primary concerns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diagnostic session scheduling">
    We agree on a time and format (on-site or remote) for the structured diagnostic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diagnostic delivery">
    ByteWise conducts the session and delivers a written findings report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Strategic plan">
    Based on findings, we develop a prioritized roadmap tailored to your institution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to prepare for your first conversation

You don't need to prepare anything technical. It helps to have a sense of: your institution type and size (number of students, staff), any specific concerns or recent incidents you're aware of, and who in your organization is responsible for technology decisions. ByteWise will guide the rest.
