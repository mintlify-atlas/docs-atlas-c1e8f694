## Why ByteWise

Educational institutions are no longer immune to the threats that affect enterprise organizations. Schools manage sensitive student and financial data, rely on increasingly complex technology systems, and are adopting AI tools — often without a clear strategy or the security posture to support them.

ByteWise was built specifically for this context. We don't sell generic technology solutions. We start by understanding your institution's unique situation, identify your real risks and gaps, and deliver a practical plan you can act on immediately.
