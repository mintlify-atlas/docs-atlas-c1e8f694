A plan is only valuable if it gets executed. ByteWise stays with your institution through implementation — not just handing over a document. Our phased approach ensures changes are manageable, teams are prepared, and progress is visible at every step.

## The four-phase engagement model

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deep diagnostic">
    Map your current state, identify risks and gaps, and establish a clear baseline. [Learn more about the diagnostic](/methodology/diagnostic).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Strategic plan">
    Deliver a prioritized, actionable roadmap tailored to your institution's budget, staff capacity, and risk tolerance. [Learn more about the strategic plan](/methodology/strategic-plan).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phased implementation">
    Execute improvements in structured phases, starting with quick wins and critical fixes. Each phase has defined deliverables, timelines, and success criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continuous accompaniment">
    Ongoing advisory support to monitor progress, address new issues, and adjust the plan as your institution evolves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What phased implementation means

Rather than attempting a full transformation at once, ByteWise breaks improvements into manageable phases. Each phase has clear deliverables, timelines, and success criteria. Your institution retains control at every step — changes are validated with your team before moving forward, and no phase begins until the previous one is stable.

## What you can expect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does an engagement last?">
    Engagements typically begin with a diagnostic and strategic plan, which takes 1–4 weeks depending on your institution's size and complexity. The implementation phase runs 3–12 months depending on scope. Accompaniment is ongoing and continues as long as your institution needs advisory support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who do we work with at the institution?">
    ByteWise works with whoever is responsible for technology decisions — this could be the principal, administrator, IT coordinator, or a combination. We adapt to your structure and make sure the right people are involved at each stage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if we already have some IT support?">
    ByteWise complements existing IT staff or vendors. We focus on strategy, security, and risk management — not day-to-day IT support. In most cases, ByteWise and your existing IT provider work in parallel without conflict.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is this disruptive to school operations?">
    The engagement is designed to be non-disruptive. Sessions are scheduled around your calendar, and changes are implemented in a way that minimizes impact on classes and administration. Where disruption is unavoidable, it is planned and communicated in advance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  ByteWise's goal is to make itself progressively less necessary — by building your institution's internal capability and leaving you with processes and tools you can maintain independently.
</Note>
