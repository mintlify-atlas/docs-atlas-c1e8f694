## Why education-specific cybersecurity matters

Generic enterprise security frameworks don't map well to educational environments. ByteWise understands the specific constraints schools face: shared devices across classrooms, seasonal staff turnover, parent communication systems with broad access, and student data regulations that differ from commercial data protection requirements. Our assessments are scoped to these realities, not adapted from frameworks built for banks or hospitals.

<Tip>
  Even a basic security assessment can uncover critical gaps. Most schools we work with have never had an external security review.
</Tip>
