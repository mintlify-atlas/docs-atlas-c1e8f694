## Common infrastructure gaps we find

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No device inventory or lifecycle plan">
    Schools often don't know how many devices they have, their ages, or when they need replacement. Without this visibility, budgeting is reactive and equipment failures are unpredictable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unsegmented networks">
    Student Wi-Fi and administrative systems running on the same network create unnecessary risk. A compromised student device can potentially reach sensitive staff or financial systems.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Outdated or unlicensed software">
    Common in schools due to budget pressure, outdated or unlicensed software creates both security vulnerabilities and compliance risk — particularly for administrative platforms handling student data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No backup strategy">
    Critical data is often stored only on local devices without regular, tested backups. A single hardware failure or ransomware incident can result in permanent data loss.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
